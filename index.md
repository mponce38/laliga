# La Liga – Top Spanish Football Clubs

La Liga is the top professional football division in Spain and one of the most popular leagues in the world.  
This website highlights three of the most successful and well-known clubs in La Liga.

---

## FC Barcelona

![FC Barcelona Logo](fcb.png)

FC Barcelona, commonly known as Barça, was founded in 1899 and is based in Barcelona, Spain.  
The club is known for its attacking style of play and its famous youth academy, La Masia.

**Notable facts:**
- Stadium: Camp Nou  
- Colors: Blue and Red  
- Famous players: Lionel Messi, Xavi, Iniesta  

---

## Real Madrid

![Real Madrid Logo](rm.png)
Real Madrid is one of the most successful football clubs in history.  
Founded in 1902, the club has won numerous domestic and international titles.

**Notable facts:**
- Stadium: Santiago Bernabéu  
- Colors: White  
- Famous players: Cristiano Ronaldo, Zidane, Raúl  

---

## Atlético Madrid

![Atlético Madrid Logo](am.png)

Atlético Madrid was founded in 1903 and is known for its strong defense and disciplined style of play.  
The club has grown into one of the top competitors in La Liga.

**Notable facts:**
- Stadium: Civitas Metropolitano  
- Colors: Red and White  
- Famous players: Diego Forlán, Antoine Griezmann
